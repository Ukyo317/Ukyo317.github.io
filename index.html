Definition of Federated Learning

Privacy of Federated Learning

SMC

SMC: zero knowledge, efficient, lower security requirement
e.g. Sharemind
MPC: no revealing sensitive data
3PC: an honest majority and consider security in both semi-honest and malicious assumptions. Requires participants’ data to be secretly-shared among non-colluding servers

Differential Privacy

differential privacy, k-anonymity,and diversification involve in adding noise to the data,or using generalization methods to obscure certain sensitive attributes until the third party cannot distinguish the individual
e.g. [23] add protection to client-side data by hiding client’s contributions during training.

Homomorphic Encryption

the data and the model itself are not transmitted
so, little possibility of leakage at the raw data level
e.g. Additively Homomorphic Encryption
polynomial approximations need to be made to evaluate non-linear functions
