Papers:

1. Federated Machine Learning: Concepts and Applications

limits:
data exists in the form of isolated islands
industry competition, privacy security and complicated administrative procedures

Definition of Federated Learning

| V(FED) - V(SUM) | < δ: δ-accuracy loss

Privacy of Federated Learning

SMC

SMC: zero knowledge, efficient, lower security requirement
e.g. Sharemind
MPC: no revealing sensitive data
3PC: an honest majority and consider security in both semi-honest and malicious assumptions. Requires participants’ data to be secretly-shared among non-colluding servers

Differential Privacy

differential privacy, k-anonymity,and diversification involve in adding noise to the data,or using generalization methods to obscure certain sensitive attributes until the third party cannot distinguish the individual
e.g. [23] add protection to client-side data by hiding client’s contributions during training.

Homomorphic Encryption

the data and the model itself are not transmitted
so, little possibility of leakage at the raw data level
e.g. Additively Homomorphic Encryption
polynomial approximations need to be made to evaluate non-linear functions

Indirect information leakage

backdoors, loopholes

Blockchain

BlockFL(block-chained federated learning) architecture

A Categorization of Federated Learning

Federated Learning for Mobile Keyboard Prediction

old method:

FST
searching for the highest-order n-gram state that matches the preceding text
Primary language model for the English language: a Katz smoothed Bayesian interpolated 5-gram LM containing 1.25 million n-grams, including 164000 unigrams.

demand:

models should be small and inference-time latency should be low.
language models are usually limited to tens of megabytes in size with vocabularies of hundreds of thousands of words because of client device batteries
